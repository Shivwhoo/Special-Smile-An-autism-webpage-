SPECIAL SMILE CENTER - CLIENT PAGES ARCHITECTURE SUMMARY
=========================================================

Below is a detailed overview of the 10 core JSX page files located under the "client/src/pages/" directory, which drive the routing and user experience of the Special Smile web application.

1. Home.jsx (Landing Page)
--------------------------
- Purpose: The primary landing page for the clinic.
- Features: 
  * Sleek hero section with a primary CTA leading to consultation scheduling.
  * Overview of the clinic's specialized developmental therapies.
  * Integrated introduction of Dr. Lovely Priya (Lead Consultant & Founder) using her verified portrait image ("/image.png") and rating statistics.
  * Informational layout on developmental milestones and parent testimonials.

2. Services.jsx (Therapies & Offerings)
---------------------------------------
- Purpose: Showcases all therapeutic specializations offered.
- Features:
  * Lists clinical details for: Special Education, Behavioral Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech & Language Therapy, Sensory Integration, Counseling, and Social Skills.
  * Provides a completely fee-free layout (all payment summaries removed).
  * Specifies session timings with a clean "(tentative)" suffix to manage schedules flexibly.

3. Mentor.jsx (About Dr. Lovely Priya)
--------------------------------------
- Purpose: Dedicated professional bio page for the founder.
- Features:
  * Renders Dr. Lovely Priya's professional portrait ("/image.png").
  * Outlines her unique medical background (B.D.S.) combined with special education qualifications.
  * Displays a custom milestone statistic: "Through my own experience" under her portrait.
  * Renders educational credentials, core philosophy ("My Approach"), and operational hours.

4. BookAppointment.jsx (Interactive Appointment Scheduler)
---------------------------------------------------------
- Purpose: Highly functional, interactive page for authenticated users to book sessions.
- Features:
  * Custom Month-switching Grid Calendar: Highlights current date, renders weekdays, and disables past dates.
  * Live Slot Query: Selecting a date queries the server "/api/appointments/booked-slots?date=YYYY-MM-DD" dynamically.
  * Available Slot Selection: Renders active timings as interactive pills, disabling already booked slots to prevent double-bookings.
  * Direct Confirmation Flow: Clean summary cards showing therapist, Patna center location, and session time. Bypasses all payment gateways, saving events directly into MongoDB and Google Calendar.

5. Dashboard.jsx (Parent & Patient Portal)
------------------------------------------
- Purpose: Protected portal for parents to view and manage active bookings.
- Features:
  * Fetches and presents a chronological list of active, pending, or past appointments.
  * Shows booking parameters (date, slot, child name, and status).
  * Displays warning notices if Google Calendar service accounts are unconfigured, while retaining local clinic booking features.

6. Login.jsx (Parent Authentication Portal)
-------------------------------------------
- Purpose: Allows registered parents to log in securely.
- Features:
  * Form fields for email and password.
  * Connects to the Redux authentication slice to dispatch secure login states and handles secure cookie-based session responses.

7. Register.jsx (Onboarding & Registration)
-------------------------------------------
- Purpose: New account onboarding flow for parents.
- Features:
  * Onboards parent contact details (name, email, phone) alongside child information.
  * Implements smooth validation feedback and redirects to the Booking Portal upon successful creation.

8. Contact.jsx (Support & Center Locations)
-------------------------------------------
- Purpose: Complete contact portal for Special Smile Center.
- Features:
  * Address: Adarsh Vihar Colony, Lane No. 1, T-Point, Gola Road, Patna, Bihar.
  * Real interactive Google Maps iframe pinpointing the Patna center.
  * Clickable redirection link that jumps directly to Google Maps navigation (maps.app.goo.gl/P48Qe5iVSdP3yf8k6).
  * Integrated contact triggers: "tel:" and "mailto:" (jaylovelypriya@gmail.com) links.
  * Stylized layout containing an on-page email inquiry form.

9. ComingSoon.jsx (Feature Placeholders)
----------------------------------------
- Purpose: Reusable placeholder component for upcoming sections.
- Features:
  * Rendered dynamically for: "About Us", "FAQs", "Parent Resources", "Privacy Policy", and "Terms of Service".
  * Displays a gorgeous high-contrast coming soon animation with secondary navigation prompts.

10. NotFound.jsx (404 Error Catch-all)
--------------------------------------
- Purpose: Fallback page for unmatched web requests.
- Features:
  * Displays an engaging, parent-friendly error card informing them the page does not exist.
  * Renders a clean home action button to redirect them back to the active web layout.
